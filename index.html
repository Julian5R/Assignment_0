Awesome and Adorable Facts About Splatoon!

There are 3 mainline Splatoon games: Splatoon, Splatoon 2, and Splatoon 3.


In order to play the game, you must choose a main weapon. In Splatoon 3, there are 11 main weapon classes: Shooters, rollers, chargers, sloshers, splatings, dualies, brellas, blasters, brushes, stringers, and splatanas. Each main weapon is paired with a sub and speacial weapon.
